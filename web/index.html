How Claro Helps

📷

Scan Documents

Take a photo of any immigration document. Claro reads the text using on-device OCR — no cloud, no uploads, completely private.

💬

Plain-Language Explanations

Get a clear summary of what the document says and what it means for you — in English, Spanish, or Haitian Creole.

🔔

Deadline Reminders

Claro detects important dates — court hearings, filing deadlines — and sets reminders so you never miss them.

🛡️

Scam Detection

Documents are checked against known scam patterns and notario fraud. Claro warns you if something looks suspicious.

📞

Verified Legal Help

Get connected to real, verified organizations — legal aid, hotlines, and advocacy groups in South Florida, Texas, Arizona, and nationwide.

🔒

100% On-Device

Everything stays on your phone. No accounts, no servers, no data collection. Your documents are yours alone.

Your Privacy is Not Negotiable

Claro was built by and for immigrant communities. We understand the stakes. That's why the app processes everything locally on your device — no internet connection needed, no data sent anywhere, no tracking. Period.
